Professional Journey

I’ve led major shifts in systems, roles, and structures — from ERP implementations to cross-site portfolio setups and operational excellence programs. In doing so, I translate ambiguity into clarity, resistance into alignment, and vision into execution. I’m most effective where transformation must become reality — with focus, rhythm, and resilience.
