About Me / Über mich

I’m Karsten Zenk – a strategic thinker, tactical shaper, and operational partner. I combine engineering roots with executive project capabilities, always focusing on creating measurable value across people, processes, and purpose.

Over three decades, I’ve transitioned from mastering machinery and plant operations to leading complex transformation projects in regulated industries. I bring together hands-on experience, structured thinking, and agile mindset to realize solutions that work — technically, economically, and humanely.

My mission? To help organizations unlock operational clarity, reduce complexity, and align execution with strategic intent — sustainably, collaboratively, and with lasting impact.
