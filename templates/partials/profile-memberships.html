Professional Memberships

I’m an active member of three international organizations that shape professional standards and knowledge exchange in systems and project leadership:

  • PMI – Project Management Institute
    Global standard-setter for project, program, and portfolio management
  • INCOSE – International Council on Systems Engineering
    Thought leader in systems thinking, lifecycle engineering, and interdisciplinary collaboration
  • GFSE – Gesellschaft für Systems Engineering
    German hub for systems engineering methods, tools, and practice

These memberships connect me to practitioners, methods, and insights across industries and continents. They strengthen the way I design solutions – grounded in standards, informed by experience, and aligned with real-world challenges.
